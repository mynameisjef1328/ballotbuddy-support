Privacy Policy

Ballot Buddy is designed to be simple and privacy-first.

What Ballot Buddy collects

Nothing. Ballot Buddy does not require an account, and it does not collect your name, email, address, party affiliation, or any other personal data.

External links

Ballot Buddy opens reputable third-party resources in your browser. Those sites may request information (for example, an address to locate a polling place). Any information you enter on those sites is handled by them, not by Ballot Buddy.

Analytics and tracking

Ballot Buddy does not include in-app analytics SDKs or advertising trackers.

Data storage

Ballot Buddy does not store personal data. If you host this privacy page on a website (GitHub Pages or similar), the hosting provider may collect standard server logs (like IP address and user agent) as part of normal website operation.

Contact

For privacy questions, email: builtbyeljefe@gmail.com

Not affiliated

Ballot Buddy is not affiliated with any government agency. It provides quick links to reputable voting resources.

Ballot Buddy opens trusted resources in your browser, it does not collect personal data.
